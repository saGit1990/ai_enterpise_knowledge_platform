
This is a sample enterprise policy document.

The company requires all employees to follow information security rules.
Confidential documents must not be shared outside approved systems.
Employees must report suspicious activity to the security team.
Vendor onboarding requires approval from procurement, legal, and compliance teams.
All contracts must include data privacy clauses and termination conditions.
Access to sensitive systems must follow role-based access control.
